My Education Journey - Mr. Jayson KUNI

My education journey from childhood up till these days has not been that too easy as people may presume. To be honest to myself and to the Great Lord above, I am for most one the toughest survivor of all time.
I have sacrificed my only life just to get myself educated. I did not think I would make it to the university either at my lower grades. During those times, I could hustle myself going from one place to another as nomad looking for opportunities to see if I could be fortunate to find possibilities.
I come from Kambiya village, a locality situated at the upper edge of Marient LLG of Kandep District in Enga Province. Over the century, Kandep has known to be one of the worst, and devasted district in Enga with its tribal herosim and warlords roaring everywhere. This ongoing situation does not spare a priviledge on us the upcoming kids of the valley. We have to look for schools, health cares, job opportunities and other basic services elsewhere where we find safe.
Due to that, I did not settle well at one setting and at least complete a chapter in education there. Wherever I went, trouble fighting could force me leave broken hearted. And sometimes I had to repeat the same grade.
